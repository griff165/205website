Welcome to Finer Bakes

The home of finely crafted cakes and cupcakes, perfect for all ages and all occasions. All our recipes have been developed and perfected over the years creating a unique taste where the only way to truly appreciate them is through taste. PME certified Donnette started baking and decorating cakes from 1995 in 2007 things escalated where baking turned into a business with orders pouring in. The old ethos goes a cake is only as fine as the love that is put in.
